About LOGIFIED SOLUTIONS

LOGIFIED SOLUTIONS PVT LTD is a modern lifting solutions provider specializing in all types of lifting equipment. With a manufacturing facility of over 12,000 sq. ft. and a 30-ton/week capacity, we deliver tailored solutions backed by expert after-sales support.


Our team of experienced engineers and technicians work closely with clients to understand their unique requirements and deliver cost-effective, reliable lifting solutions. From design to installation and maintenance, we ensure complete customer satisfaction.


With years of industry experience and a commitment to quality, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses across various sectors requiring reliable lifting equipment.
